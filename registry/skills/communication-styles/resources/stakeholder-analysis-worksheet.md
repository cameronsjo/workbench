## Quick Reference During Live Interactions

### Flex Response Guide

**If AMIABLE shows stress (acquiescing):**
→ Create safe space, ask "What are your real concerns?"

**If EXPRESSIVE shows stress (attacking):**
→ Let them vent, acknowledge emotions, redirect to solutions

**If ANALYTIC shows stress (avoiding):**
→ Provide deadline with rationale, offer to answer questions

**If DRIVER shows stress (autocracy):**
→ Stand firm with facts, respect authority, focus on results

---

### Power Words Quick Reference

| Style | Top 5 Power Words |
|-------|-------------------|
| AMIABLE | Guarantee, Reliable, Tested, Safety, Team |
| EXPRESSIVE | Innovative, Exciting, Creative, Recognize, Appreciate |
| ANALYTIC | Research, Data, Evidence, Proven, Systematic |
| DRIVER | ROI, Results, Fast, Efficiency, Win |

---

### Communication Speed Guide

**FAST decisions:** Expressive, Driver
→ Be ready for quick calls, rapid responses

**SLOW decisions:** Amiable, Analytic
→ Build in time, don't rush, follow up patiently
