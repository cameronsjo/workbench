# Brand Alignment Check

## Purpose

This assessment identifies gaps between:

- How you want to be seen (aspirational brand)
- How you're currently seen (current brand)

## Scoring Interpretation

### 20-25: Strong Alignment

Your behaviors, time allocation, communications, and stakeholder perceptions strongly align with your aspirational brand. Focus on sustaining and refining.

---

### 15-19: Moderate Alignment

You have good foundation but inconsistencies exist. Focus on eliminating undermining behaviors and increasing aligned activities.

---

### 10-14: Weak Alignment

Significant gaps exist between aspiration and reality. Focus on behavior change plan and anti-brand elimination.

---

### Below 10: Misalignment

Your daily reality does not reflect your aspirational brand. Consider whether brand is realistic or behaviors need major overhaul.
