# Brand Observation Journal

Track how others perceive you through daily observations. Look for patterns over 2-4 weeks.

## Instructions

- Record observations daily or after significant interactions
- Focus on what others say and do, not what you intended
- Look for patterns across multiple observations
- Be specific - include direct quotes when possible
- Note surprises or contradictions to your self-perception
