# Influence Audit Assessment

Measure your current influence and impact - the ultimate test of executive presence.

## Purpose

Executive presence is proven through impact: what people DO because of their experiences with you. This audit assesses your current influence.

---

## The Three Phases of Executive Presence

Your influence operates through:

1. **IMAGE** - What people know and think (status, reputation)
2. **IMPRESSIONS** - What people feel (appearances, communications)
3. **IMPACT** - What people do (experiences, outcomes)

This audit focuses on **IMPACT** - the ultimate measure.

## Scoring Interpretation

### 20-25: High Influence (Strong Executive Presence)

You have significant influence and impact. People implement your recommendations, share information with you, and advocate for you. Your executive presence is strong.

**Focus:** Sustain and expand influence. Consider Parts 2-3 of executive presence (Impressions and Impact refinement).

---

### 15-19: Moderate Influence (Developing Executive Presence)

You have established influence in some areas but gaps exist. Your presence is recognized but not consistently impactful.

**Focus:** Identify influence gaps and develop brand promise that addresses them.

---

### 10-14: Limited Influence (Emerging Executive Presence)

Your influence is limited to specific situations or stakeholders. Your presence may not extend beyond your immediate role.

**Focus:** Build intentional brand, increase stakeholder engagement, demonstrate strategic value.

---

### Below 10: Minimal Influence (Need Brand Development)

You have little influence beyond your direct responsibilities. Your presence is limited or unintentional.

**Focus:** Complete full brand discovery and development process. Start with understanding current brand and market needs.
