# Brand Promise Statement Template

Your brand promise is a **2-3 word statement** that captures the distinctive value or experiences your market can expect from you.

## Core Principles

### Brand Promise Should Be

1. **Memorable and Distinctive** - Not generic or forgettable
2. **Value-Focused** - Communicates what market receives or experiences
3. **Authentic** - Aligned with your values and capabilities
4. **Evidence-Based** - Supported by your track record
5. **Differentiating** - Sets you apart from peers
6. **Professional** - Appropriate for CV, LinkedIn, bios

### Brand Promise Should NOT Be

1. **Generic role descriptors** - "IT Executive," "Product Leader"
2. **Vague qualities** - "Strategic thinker," "Team player"
3. **Aspirational without evidence** - Claims you can't demonstrate
4. **Inauthentic** - Personas you can't sustain
5. **Role expectations** - Everyone expects a CIO to be strategic

---

## Brand Promise Formula

**[Distinctive Approach/Capability] + [Value/Outcome Delivered]**

Compressed into 2-3 words maximum.
