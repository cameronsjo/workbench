## Best Practices

1. Be specific and clear about the task
2. Provide necessary context
3. Specify output format if needed
4. Use simple, direct language
5. Include constraints or requirements

## When to Use Zero-Shot

- Task is straightforward and well-defined
- Model has strong base capabilities for the task
- Speed and simplicity are priorities
- You don't have good examples available

## Limitations

- May be inconsistent for complex tasks
- Output format may vary
- May misinterpret ambiguous requests
